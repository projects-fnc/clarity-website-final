Redefining Diagnostics in Perumbavoor

Advanced technology. Experienced hands. A patient experience built on trust, precision, and care.

Precision Imaging. Personal Care.

Clariti Scan World was started with a simple idea, to bring cutting-edge diagnostic care to Perumbavoor, with warmth and integrity. We're proud to be the first and only MRI centre in the area, equipped with a 1.5 Tesla MRI that delivers clarity when it matters most.

From general imaging to fetal medicine, we combine medical expertise, state-of-the-art equipment, and a patient-first attitude to make your experience smooth, fast, and reassuring.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, accurate, and advanced diagnostic services to the community of Perumbavoor and surrounding areas, using state-of-the-art technology and a patient-centric approach.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted diagnostic center in the region, recognized for excellence in medical imaging, laboratory services, and patient care.
